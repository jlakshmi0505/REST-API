About this area

South San Francisco

Located in South San Francisco, Travelodge by Wyndham San Francisco Airport North is in an area with good shopping. About this property

Travelodge by Wyndham San Francisco Airport North

A restaurant, an outdoor pool, and a business center are available at this smoke-free motel. Free WiFi in public areas, free self parking, and a free airport shuttle are also provided. Other amenities include conference space, concierge services, and dry cleaning. All 200 rooms offer free WiFi, coffee makers, and TVs with cable channels. Hair dryers, safes, and ironing boards are among the other amenities that guests will find.
